Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I deep clean my carpets with the Hoover® SmartWash™?

We recommend deep cleaning 2-4 times per year. In addition to routine spring cleaning and holiday cleaning, SmartWash™ is great for cleaning high-traffic areas, stairs, upholstery and those inevitable spots and spills.

Can the Hoover® SmartWash™ clean furniture?

Yes! Your SmartWash™ comes with an 8-foot hose and various tools to clean stains and spills on upholstered furniture. Always check manufacturer's label before cleaning for any specific instructions. Before you clean, test a small hidden area of carpet for color fastness. If color changes, do not use.

How much cleaning solution do I need when using my SmartWash™?

The great thing about the SmartWash™ is there is NO measuring to do! Simply fill the Solution Tank with Hoover® cleaning solution and the SmartWash™ takes care of all the mixing and measuring, it's that easy!
